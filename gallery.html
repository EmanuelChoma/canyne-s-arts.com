About Me

Tshepang "Canyne" Monyela, born in the Limpopo province of South Africa. Art has always been a means of expression and freedom for me. The journey from a blank paper to a complete art piece is an adventure for the imagination, and to embark on that journey is casting away all burdens. It's to be free.
